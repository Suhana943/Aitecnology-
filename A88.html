AI Ethics: Addressing Algorithmic Fairness

Published: July 9, 2026 | Category: Ethics

Algorithmic fairness ensures that AI systems do not perpetuate societal biases, necessitating technical interventions and rigorous auditing processes.

Key Fairness Strategies

  • Data-Centric Interventions: Ensuring representative training sets and synthetic balancing.
  • Algorithmic Methods: Using adversarial debiasing to minimize reliance on protected attributes.
  • Bias Audits: Independent evaluations to monitor and mitigate outcome disparities.

Graph Neural Networks: Introduction

Published: July 9, 2026 | Category: Deep Learning

GNNs extend deep learning to non-Euclidean data, enabling models to learn from the complex relationships and structures inherent in networks.

Core Advantages

  • Structural Awareness: Models relationships, not just flat attributes.
  • Topological Learning: Propagates information based on graph connectivity.
  • Flexibility: Applicable to chemistry, social networks, and recommendation systems.

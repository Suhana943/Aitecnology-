Transformers: The Decoder Architecture

Published: July 9, 2026 | Category: NLP

The Transformer Decoder is specialized for sequence generation. It uses Masked Self-Attention to ensure that predictions at each step only depend on previously generated tokens.

Key Components

  • Masked Self-Attention: Prevents the model from "peeking" into the future during training.
  • Encoder-Decoder Attention: Enables the model to focus on specific input data during generation.
  • Autoregressive Generation: Iterative prediction of the next token.

Quick Example

# Define and use a decoder stack
decoder_layer = nn.TransformerDecoderLayer(d_model=512, nhead=8)
transformer_decoder = nn.TransformerDecoder(decoder_layer, num_layers=6)

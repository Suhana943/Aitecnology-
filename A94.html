Model Distillation for Lightweight Models

Published: July 9, 2026 | Category: Optimization

Model distillation compresses the intelligence of a massive teacher model into a compact student model by training the student to mimic the teacher's probability outputs.

Key Benefits

  • Efficiency: Drastically reduces model size and inference time.
  • Dark Knowledge: Learns relationships between classes via soft targets.
  • Deployment: Enables high-performance AI on mobile and edge devices.

Reinforcement Learning: Q-Learning Concepts

Published: July 9, 2026 | Category: Advanced

Q-learning teaches an agent to make optimal decisions by building a table of "Quality" values (Q-values) that estimate future rewards for every state-action pair.

Core Components

  • Q-Table: A lookup table for state-action quality scores.
  • Bellman Equation: The recursive formula used to update Q-values based on rewards and future expectations.
  • Exploration/Exploitation: The strategy for balancing new discovery vs. known optimal actions.

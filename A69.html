NLP: The Attention Mechanism Explained

Published: July 9, 2026 | Category: NLP

The Attention Mechanism allows models to dynamically weigh the importance of different parts of the input data, overcoming the limitations of fixed-length memory in earlier architectures.

Key Concepts

  • Queries, Keys, and Values: Vectors used to calculate relevance scores.
  • Softmax Normalization: Ensures attention weights sum to 1.
  • Contextual Focus: Enables models to capture long-range dependencies efficiently.

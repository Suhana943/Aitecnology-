Natural Language Processing: Text Tokenization

Published: July 9, 2026 | Category: NLP

Tokenization is the process of converting raw text into smaller units (tokens). It is the critical first step in preparing text data for deep learning models.

Common Methods

  • Word: Simple but limited by vocabulary size.
  • Character: Flexible but creates very long sequences.
  • Sub-word: The modern standard; balances vocabulary size and flexibility.

Quick Example

from transformers import AutoTokenizer

tokenizer = AutoTokenizer.from_pretrained("bert-base-uncased")
tokens = tokenizer.tokenize("Tokenization is essential for NLP.")
ids = tokenizer.convert_tokens_to_ids(tokens)

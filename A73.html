Generative AI: Foundations of GPT

Published: July 9, 2026 | Category: GenAI

GPT models are decoder-only Transformers trained to predict the next token in a sequence. By scaling this task across massive datasets and fine-tuning with human feedback, they act as powerful, generative agents.

Key Concepts

  • Autoregressive Generation: Predicts tokens one by one, feeding them back as context.
  • Self-Supervised Learning: Learns general patterns from vast text data.
  • Alignment (RLHF): Refines the raw model into a helpful assistant.

Quick Example

from transformers import pipeline
generator = pipeline('text-generation', model='gpt2')
print(generator("The future of AI is", max_length=30))

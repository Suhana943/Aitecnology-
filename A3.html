Understanding Computer Vision: Theory and Deep Learning Foundations

Published on: July 7, 2026 | By: Aitecnology Team

Computer Vision, Artificial Intelligence (AI) ka ek bahut hi mahatvapurn kshetra hai jo computers ko hamari tarah 'dekhne' aur duniya ko samajhne ki shamta deta hai. Is article mein hum dekhenge ki yeh technology kaise kaam karti hai.

Computer Vision kya hai?

Computer Vision ka matlab hai machines ko digital images ya videos se meaningful information nikalna sikhana. Jab hum camera se koi tasveer lete hain, toh computer use sirf pixel numbers ki tarah dekhta hai. Lekin Deep Learning ki madad se, computer us tasveer ke andar maujud objects (jaise insaan, gaadi, ya jaanwar) ko pehchan sakta hai.

Yeh kaise kaam karta hai?

Computer Vision ka process kuch mukhya steps mein hota hai:

  • Acquiring: Sensors ya cameras ke zariye visual data collect karna.
  • Processing: Data ko saaf aur computer ke samajhne layak banana.
  • Analyzing: Algorithms (khaaskar Convolutional Neural Networks) ka use karke features dhundna.
  • Understanding: Pehchane gaye patterns ke aadhar par faisla lena ya result dena.

Aitecnology Tip: Computer Vision aajkal autonomous cars, facial recognition, aur medical imaging jaise bade fields mein revolution la raha hai. Ise samajhna AI career ke liye bahut zaruri hai.

Reinforcement Learning: Agent and Environment

Published: July 9, 2026 | Category: Advanced

Reinforcement Learning is about teaching an agent to navigate an environment through rewards and penalties, optimizing its decision-making policy over time.

Key Components

  • Agent: The learner or decision-maker.
  • Environment: The world the agent interacts with.
  • Policy: The strategy for choosing actions.

Quick Example

# Basic RL interaction loop with Gymnasium
action = env.action_space.sample()
next_state, reward, terminated, _, _ = env.step(action)

Advanced Audio Synthesis Technology

Published: July 9, 2026 | Category: NLP

Neural Audio Synthesis replaces traditional mathematical oscillators with deep learning models capable of generating highly realistic speech and soundscapes.

Core Methodologies

  • Autoregressive Models: Generate audio one sample at a time.
  • Diffusion Models: Refine noise into high-fidelity audio.
  • Neural Vocoders: Final step to convert spectrograms into audible sound.

Transformers: The Encoder Architecture

Published: July 9, 2026 | Category: NLP

The Transformer Encoder is designed to create deeply contextual representations of input sequences. By utilizing self-attention, it captures relationships between all tokens in a sequence simultaneously.

Key Components

  • Multi-Head Self-Attention: Learns different types of token dependencies in parallel.
  • Residual Connections & Norm: Essential for training deep stacks of layers.
  • Position-wise FFN: Provides the non-linear capacity for learning complex features.

Quick Example

# Define and use an encoder stack
encoder_layer = nn.TransformerEncoderLayer(d_model=512, nhead=8)
transformer_encoder = nn.TransformerEncoder(encoder_layer, num_layers=6)

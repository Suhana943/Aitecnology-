Reinforcement Learning: Policy Gradients

Published: July 9, 2026 | Category: Advanced

Policy gradient methods optimize agent behavior by directly adjusting policy parameters to increase the probability of high-reward actions.

Key Characteristics

  • Direct Optimization: Learns the policy distribution rather than value functions.
  • Continuous Actions: Ideal for robotic control and complex environments.
  • Stochastic Nature: Naturally supports probabilistic strategies for non-deterministic environments.

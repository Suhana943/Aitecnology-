AI Infrastructure: Scalable GPU Training

Published: July 9, 2026 | Category: Advanced

Scaling AI model training involves distributing compute-heavy workloads across massive GPU clusters, requiring sophisticated parallelization and high-speed interconnects.

Scaling Paradigms

  • Data Parallelism: Distributing batches across multiple replicas.
  • Model Parallelism: Splitting layers/tensors across devices for memory efficiency.
  • Pipeline Parallelism: Staging layer computation to maximize throughput.

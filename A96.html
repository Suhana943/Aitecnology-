Robotics: Robotic Grasping Mechanisms

Published: July 9, 2026 | Category: Robotics

Robotic grasping bridges the gap between perception and physical interaction, utilizing everything from simple parallel jaws to complex, tactile-enabled soft hands.

Grasping Strategies

  • Parallel-Jaw: Efficient, high-reliability, standard industrial choice.
  • Multi-Fingered: Highly dexterous, mimics human-like manipulation.
  • Soft/Compliant: Naturally adapts to unknown or delicate geometries.

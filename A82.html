Reinforcement Learning: PPO Algorithm

Published: July 9, 2026 | Category: Advanced

Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) is a stable and efficient reinforcement learning algorithm that utilizes a clipped objective function to prevent erratic policy updates.

PPO Core Concepts

  • Clipped Objective: Limits the probability ratio update to a range [0.8, 1.2], ensuring stability.
  • Actor-Critic: Uses a dual-network approach to evaluate actions and estimate state values simultaneously.
  • RLHF Foundation: Widely used in modern LLM fine-tuning to align model behavior with human preferences.

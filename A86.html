Edge AI: Model Quantization Strategies

Published: July 9, 2026 | Category: Hardware

Quantization compresses deep learning models by reducing numerical precision, enabling deployment on resource-constrained edge devices with minimal accuracy loss.

Key Quantization Techniques

  • Post-Training Quantization (PTQ): Efficient conversion after training; fast and simple.
  • Quantization-Aware Training (QAT): Modeling precision loss during training for superior accuracy.
  • Scale/Zero-Point Mapping: Mathematical normalization to map floating-point to integer ranges.

Privacy Preserving Federated Learning

Published: July 9, 2026 | Category: Ethics

Federated Learning allows models to learn from decentralized data, while cryptographic techniques ensure that individual privacy remains protected throughout the process.

Key Privacy Pillars

  • Differential Privacy: Adding noise to prevent data reconstruction.
  • Secure Multi-Party Computation: Encrypting updates to hide individual contributions.
  • Data Decentralization: Ensuring raw data never leaves the user device.

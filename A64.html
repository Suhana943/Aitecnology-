Transfer Learning for Computer Vision Tasks

Published: July 9, 2026 | Category: CV

Transfer learning allows you to take a model trained on a massive dataset and apply its knowledge to your specific task, drastically reducing the need for large training datasets and long compute times.

Key Approaches

  • Feature Extraction: Freezing pre-trained weights and only training a new classifier layer.
  • Fine-Tuning: Unfreezing top layers of the pre-trained model to adapt features to your data.

Quick Example

# Load model and freeze parameters
model = models.resnet50(pretrained=True)
for param in model.parameters():
    param.requires_grad = False

# Add custom classification head
model.fc = nn.Linear(model.fc.in_features, num_classes)
